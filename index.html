about us

We make packaging work for your brand!

Doulcee Packaging Supplies was established in 2021 changed to FRAMEL PACKAGE-READY CO LTD in 2023.
The company is located on off Mombasa Road, opp. city Cabanas behind Ramtons Nairobi, Kenya.
We specialize in designing, manufacturing and distributing a wide range of packaging and
supply products including but not limited to Corrugated Cardboard boxes, S.F.K rolls and Cardboard Corners.
Our products are designed to meet the diverse needs of various industries,including retail, e-commerce,
logistics, manufacturing, and more. Framel Package-Ready Co ltd is one of the
fastest growing supplies of corrugated cardboard boxes in Kenya. We pride ourselves on
delivering excellent customer service. Our team of experienced professionals is
committed to understanding our clients' unique requirements and providing tailored solutions that meet
their specific needs. We work closely with our clients to ensure that they receive the best possible
service and that their orders are processed and delivered on time.

FAQ

How much will it cost?

To get a better idea of the cost of the products and services you need, give us a quick phone call.
We’ll ask you a few questions about the nature of the products, what sort of interactivity would you like to have,
Then we’ll be able to give you a ballpark figure. If you’re still interested, we’ll come to your place of business and come up with a firm quote.

+

How long it takes to deliver

The time limit of any order is normally dictated by the client. If you have any time limit in mind we will attempt
to assemble it for you. The main general delay in the delivery process is waiting for the client to make an order.
